Vegan Green Goddess
Green Goddess Grain Bowl
10 min Easy Serves 2
340
cal
12g
protein
48g
carbs
10g
fat
Serving size
2
Ingredients
1 × Super Sauced Vegan Green Goddess sauce
1 cup cooked farro or quinoa
1 cup mixed greens
½ cucumber, sliced
¼ avocado, sliced
2 tbsp pumpkin seeds
Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions
  1. Cook farro or quinoa according to package instructions and allow to cool slightly.
  2. Arrange greens, cucumber, and avocado in a bowl over the grains.
  3. Squeeze or spoon the Vegan Green Goddess sauce evenly over the bowl.
  4. Scatter pumpkin seeds on top, season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.
